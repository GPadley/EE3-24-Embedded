Why MagnoSpeed?

As a connected bike speedometer, MagnoSpeed enables you to measure you bike speed wherever you are, sending information to your phone and is also accessible from your computer at home. You can view your activity with ease, allowing you to implement a tight training regime.

As GPS is not being used, you can now upload your bike speed and total distace travelled to the Internet and share them with your friends, at the same time without exposing your location. Both Metric and Imperial units are available. Click to learn more about the device.
