Why MagnoSpeed?

As a connected bike speedometer, MagnoSpeed enables you to measure you bike speed wherever you are, sending information to your phone and is also accessible from your computer at home. You can view your activity with ease, allowing you to implement a tight training regime.

Since GPS is not being used, you can now upload your bike speed and total distace travelled to the Internet and share them with your friends, at the same time without exposing your location. Both Metric and Imperial units are available. Click to learn more about the device.

At Home, On the Road, And In The Gym

MangoSpeed offers your the ability to see real time data on your performance while moving, at home or in a training situation. See your speed and distance travelled appear in front of you as you cycle on either our mobile app or web Dashbaord.

By tracking your history and making use of the inbuilt training mechanisms, you can develop your stamina and sprint speeds to further your development as a cyclist.

Designed for User-Ease from the Ground Up

Taking less than a minute to install on your bike, and a matter of seconds to get started, there is no simplar way to record your bike jounrneys than with MangoSpeed.

Simply clip the reader to your bike frame and stick the magents to the spokes. That's it, your ready to go. No faffing with cables, calibrating or tools you don't own.

When you want to ride, just open the app and press start. You can close the app, turn off your phone or even leave it at home.
