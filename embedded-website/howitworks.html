How does it work?

The basic operation of the device

The Magnetometer

The deivce utilises a magnetometer to sense any change in a magnetic field. Our idea is as the magnet moves around, it creates a change in flux which is then read by the magnetometer, and thus gives us a reading in the x, y, and z direction. The microcontroller is then used to analyze the three readings and uses to make a conclusion about the speed of the bike. In our case, we are attaching it onto a bicycle and use the data to determine the instantaneous speed, as well as the total distance travelled.

Originally, the magnetometer can give out a varying set of data without any external reference due to the Earth's magnetic field. However, after operating a long period of time, the data will start to shift to a different absolute value due to other stronger magnetic forces in the environment. As a result, a magnet is attached to the bicycle, and with it, the device now gives out a more reliable set of values.

After collecting the data, analyzing it and drawing a conclusion, this is then transmitted to the phone. You are then able to view your travel time, speed, average speed, maximum speed so far and distance travelled using our app. The data is also transmitted to the website, which when you log into your account, you are able to see your speed against time of your trips plotted on a graph. This data is stored and you can see how your speed varied across previous journeys.

The Software

We have implemented the cutting-edge technology of JSON and MQTT to transfer data from the microcontroller on board to the Internet.
